My story began on December 14 in Manila, Philippines, but my path has always been guided by a pull toward home and a drive for the future. While I spent my first decade in Bacoor, Cavite, life took a meaningful turn when I was ten. My parents decided to return to their origins in Davao City, and in doing so, they brought me back to our family roots. Growing up in Davao wasn't just about a new city; it was about reconnecting with a legacy that shaped my identity and gave me a steady foundation for the years to come. In 2019, I made the bold move to Cebu, a city known for its vibrant energy and professional opportunity. I came here seeking career growth, a journey from Japanese-English translation that has evolved into a dedicated pursuit of technology. Today, I am an aspiring Data Engineer and Web Developer, balancing the logic of data structures with the creativity of the web. I am fascinated by the power of information and the ability to build digital spaces from the ground up—a reflection of the way I have carefully built my own life. The year 2024 stands as a landmark of personal joy, marking the year I married my partner and solidified the family we are building together. My days are now a beautiful, busy blend of technical ambition and motherhood. With two daughters—a four-year-old and a nine-month-old—I am constantly reminded of why I strive for excellence. Whether I am debugging code, maintaining a data pipeline, or navigating the wonderful chaos of raising two young children, my life is a testament to the journey between islands and the balance between tradition and innovation. From my parents' roots in Davao to the digital frontiers I am exploring in Cebu, I am crafting a legacy of my own—one byte and one memory at a time.
