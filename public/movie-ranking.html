The Best Movies According to Patrice Mae

My top 3 movies of all-time in no particular order.

The Martian

Because I'm curious. What will an astronaut do if they're left alone in a planet that's not Earth?

Interstellar

Really cool sci-fi movie that I had to watch several times. And my goodness, the tesseract.
Don't let me spoil it. Watch it and be mind-bended like me.

The Cat Returns (猫の恩返し)

A cute anime movie that my daughter had on loop for weeks.
